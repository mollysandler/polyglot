Allow microphone for Polyglot

Polyglot needs microphone access to translate your speech in real time. Click the button below and choose Allow when Chrome asks.

If clicking Allow Microphone doesn't show a Chrome prompt, your browser may have a remembered "Block" decision for this extension. Open chrome://settings/content/microphone, find the entry under "Not allowed to use your microphone" that starts with chrome-extension://, remove it, then come back and try again.
